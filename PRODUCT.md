# PRODUCT.md

## Product Purpose
Internal design system and presentation template for Selected Digital Marketing Agency (סלקטד שיווק אינטרנטי). Used for client-facing brief summaries and internal stakeholder presentations. Primary language: Hebrew (RTL).

## Register
brand

## Users
Israeli digital marketing professionals presenting to business owners and internal managers. Viewers expect professionalism and clarity; they are business-oriented, not technical.

## Brand
- Agency: Selected Digital Marketing Agency
- Personality: Confident, results-driven, modern, no-nonsense
- Tone: Direct and authoritative — not warm-fluffy, not cold-corporate
- Anti-references: generic SaaS blue, startup pastel minimalism, cluttered infographic style

## Visual Identity
- Primary: warm orange — the brand color, used boldly and unapologetically
- Dark: near-black for dramatic contrast on cover and headers
- White/light: clean content areas
- Typography: heavy weight for titles, light for supporting text — high contrast hierarchy
- Signature element: split-panel cover (dark visual left, orange title right — RTL aware)
- Icons/accents: filled orange squares, not outlines
- Cards on content slides: information rows with orange filled icon squares, not bordered cards
